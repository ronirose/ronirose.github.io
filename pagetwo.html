Mission Statement:

Here at It's a Girl Thing!®, we believe that there is no such thing as "The Average Woman" and we embrace it! Each girl and woman has her own unique passions and abilities, and we're here to feed that passion, to empower those girls and women, and to give them the freedom they deserve to style themselves in a way that showcases just how AWESOME they are!





Our History:

  • Founded in 2014 by Roni Rose
  • Started as a local out-of-the-garage store
  • Began with the mission of empowering women by giving them the freedom to design their own clothing

Founder & CEO:

Roni Rose

Community Involvement:
